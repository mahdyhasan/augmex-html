About Augmex Technologies

Connecting Global Businesses withBangladesh's Finest Talent

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between exceptional Bangladeshi talent and global business opportunities, Augmex Technologies has become a trusted partner for companies worldwide seeking top-tier staff augmentation solutions.

Our Story

Augmex Technologies was born from a simple yet powerful observation: Bangladesh possesses an incredible pool of talented, educated, and ambitious professionals, yet this potential remains largely untapped by the global market.

Since our inception, we've been dedicated to changing this narrative. Our journey began with a small team of passionate individuals who believed in the transformative power of connecting the right talent with the right opportunities.

Today, we pride ourselves on having placed over 2,000 professionals across 15+ countries, building lasting partnerships with businesses ranging from innovative startups to Fortune 500 companies.
